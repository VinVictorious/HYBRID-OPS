1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how HYBRID OPS ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our progressive web app ("Service").

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and fitness goals.

Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you use the Service, including workout completion, progress data, and app interactions.

Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system, and browser type.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy or as required by law.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your workout data is primarily stored locally on your device using browser storage. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage and similar technologies to store your preferences, workout data, and app settings on your device. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync or backup your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated effective date.
